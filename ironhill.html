BREW HOUSES

The Brew culture in India took off in early 2000s with the launch of the first-ever microbrewery, redefining the craft experience. The birth of microbreweries shifted narrative around beer and its consumption. Cities like Gurgaon, Bengaluru, Pune and Bombay brought in a new perspective to beer with flavourful experimental craft brews, moving the crowd away from low quality mass produced bottled beer.

In the South, Prost Brew pub started this wave by introducing Bengaluru and Hyderabad to new world of beer and food experiences. Ironhill Brewery was launched in 2017 with the mission to introduce the beer aficionados to something larger than life. As a pion1eer in microbreweries, Ironhill India is now in 4 popular cities serving thousands of patrons every day.

Ironhill is set to reignite a new culture beginning in Craft brews by going national.
